"The College Tours of Boston trip gave me horrifying flashbacks to the college application process, but it is hard to deny how beautiful the campuses are here. We flew over Boston University, Northeastern University, Berklee College of Music, and drove over the bridge connecting MIT and Cambridge to the rest of Boston. The funny thing is some high school students on the trip were clearly using the tour as an opportunity to take notes on the campuses and if they liked them or not. Whether you're applying or not, however, this is a really cool experience."
