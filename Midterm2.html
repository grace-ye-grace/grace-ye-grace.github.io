About

Our mission is to provide a fun experience to visitors of all kinds - whether from Boston or from abroad - using the unique patented Cicada Tour Bus. Unlike other tour buses, the Cicada Tour Bus can fly, providing an unforgettable aerial experience of Boston. You can see the best of Boston from all angles, and the bus floats down to designated Cicada Bus landing spots to give participants a close up of famous Boston locations, like the Museum of Science, Fenway Park, or the Prudential Center. Additionally, if you find a place you just can't miss, the fare-by-location model allows you to get off at the right spot while still getting your money's worth, as you will be instantly refunded for the percent of the trip you have left. If you are interested in a trip that can take you through land, sea, and sky, then we are the only ones that can provide that experience.
